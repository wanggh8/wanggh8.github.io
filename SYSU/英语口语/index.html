Hello, everyone. It is my pleasure to have the opportunity for this interview. First, I would like to introduce myself briefly. I am XXX, coming from a beautiful city, Luo He, Henan Province. I graduated from Anyang Institute Of Technology, majoring in information management and information system.

With my effort during my four years of study, I was granted National Encouragement scholarship and Principle’s scholarship twice. What’s more, I won the first provincial prize in China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling and the second provincial prize in The Chinese Mathematics Competitions. I joined the college student union and obtained the Excellent Student Cadre. I also did part-time jobs in my spare time.

When I finished my study, I realized that what I have learned is not enough. I would like to further my study, so I choose to continue my interest with a master’s degree. If I am lucky enough to be a student of this great university, I will try my best to develop my abilities in related aspects and spare no effort to be a qualified graduate student. Thank you for your time and attention.
